About

I’m Tara McAllister and I’m an Interaction Design student at the Belfast School of Art. I’m from the sticks - also known as County Tyrone. I drink tea (milk and three sugars - I know) and eat pizza.

I work with amazing people with creative visions. Some of the things that matter most to me are: accessibility, art and learning. I’ve spent 77.5% of my life in education - it would be quite hard if I didn’t! I improve my design and development skills everyday. I have been working in tech for over two years while studying and handling freelance projects.

I’m always interested in creating cool stuff. Got a project in mind? Check out my CV or jump right in and contact me at design@taramcallister.com

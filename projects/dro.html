DURATION

2 Weeks

FOCUS

UI and UX

WHEN

September 2018

Challenge

Create a unique product that fills a gap in the market based on three randomly selected factors of Content Delivery; friends as content, special occasions as frequency and drones as our platform.

Solution

An app designed to make gift-receiving, even when you have forgotten about the special occasion, an experience in itself. An app that allows you to send your loved ones gifts in a way they've never seen before - by drone.

After some benchmarking and looking at inspiration I started sketching both the brand and the UI. I wanted modern, minimalistic design and a unique, sans-serif typeface. I focused on creating a smooth user flow to ensure a great user experience. I also focused on chunking content into concise useful pieces in order to ensure users aren't overloaded with information.

I came up with the name Dro for the service when brainstorming names that are suitable for millenials/Gen Z (my target market) and their use of social media - a short name that is easily remembered and perfect for a hashtag.

Prototying

Moving from paper prototyping to mid-fi prototyping I focused on content, form and spacing rather than colour and imagery. This makes sure the content is first. I used Sketch to create the mid-fi prototypes and the final screens below.
